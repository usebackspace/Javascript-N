Many frogs glow in blue light, and it may be a secret, eerie language

A survey of hundreds of frogs in South America shows that far more frogs are biofluorescent than previously thought,researchers report in a preprint posted July 28 at bioRxiv.org. ghostly colors may have a role in the frogs communication with members of the same species, the scientists say.
